📍 Set your observatory location

Latitude and longitude are needed for accurate Local Sidereal Time, altitude charts, meridian-flip timing, and twilight calculations. You can change this any time in Settings.

Tip: longitude is positive east of Greenwich, negative west. Most online maps show this directly.

🔭 Equipment rigs

A rig is a saved set of equipment selections + per-rig defaults (cooler temp, gain, focuser step, focal length, PHD2 endpoint). Switch rigs in one click instead of re-selecting every device.
