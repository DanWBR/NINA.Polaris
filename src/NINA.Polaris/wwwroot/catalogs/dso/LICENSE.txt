N.I.N.A. Polaris bundled DSO catalog (dso.db)
==============================================

This SQLite database aggregates the following astronomical catalogs.
All sources are public-domain or openly licensed; Polaris itself
(MPL 2.0) does not own the underlying data. Run
`scripts/build-dso-catalog.py` to rebuild from the original sources.

-------------------------------------------------------------------
1. OpenNGC (NGC + IC complete, Messier + Caldwell cross-IDs)
-------------------------------------------------------------------
   Source:  https://github.com/mattiaverga/OpenNGC
   File:    database_files/NGC.csv
   License: Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International
            (CC BY-SA 4.0)
            https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/
   Attribution:
      "OpenNGC by Mattia Verga, licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0."

-------------------------------------------------------------------
2. Sharpless 2 (HII Regions)
-------------------------------------------------------------------
   Source:  CDS VizieR catalog VII/20/catalog
            (Sharpless 1959, "A Catalogue of HII Regions")
   License: Public domain via the CDS, Strasbourg.

-------------------------------------------------------------------
3. ARP Atlas of Peculiar Galaxies
-------------------------------------------------------------------
   Source:  CDS VizieR catalog VII/192A/arplist
            (Arp 1966, "Atlas of Peculiar Galaxies")
   License: Public domain via the CDS, Strasbourg.

-------------------------------------------------------------------
4. Abell Catalog of Planetary Nebulae
-------------------------------------------------------------------
   Source:  CDS VizieR catalog V/84/abell
            (Abell 1966)
   License: Public domain via the CDS, Strasbourg.

-------------------------------------------------------------------
5. Hickson Compact Groups
-------------------------------------------------------------------
   Source:  CDS VizieR catalog VII/213/groups
            (Hickson 1982; Hickson et al. 1989)
   License: Public domain via the CDS, Strasbourg.

-------------------------------------------------------------------
6. Abell-Corwin-Olowin (ACO) Galaxy Clusters
-------------------------------------------------------------------
   Source:  CDS VizieR catalog VII/110A/table3
            (Abell, Corwin & Olowin 1989)
   License: Public domain via the CDS, Strasbourg.

-------------------------------------------------------------------
Caldwell cross-references
-------------------------------------------------------------------
The Caldwell numbering (C1..C109, Patrick Moore 1995) is a hand-
picked assignment of existing NGC / IC entries to Caldwell numbers.
The mapping table is embedded in scripts/build-dso-catalog.py as
public-domain coordinates; the underlying objects come from
OpenNGC.
